About Me

 I'm a third-year Ph.D. student in Intelligent Data Extraction, Analysis and Applications of Remote Sensing (RSIDEA) Group. My research interest include high resolution remote sensing imagery understanding and intelligent interpretation and application of medical images.

  • 2019.9-Now           Ph.D. in LIESMARS, Wuhan University. Supervisor: Prof. Yanfei Zhong & Liangpei Zhang
